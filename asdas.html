Times Square is not only known for it’s amazing bright lights and loud commotion, but is also known for it’s great food.
Times square is home to more than 300 restaurants which all have meals that are to die for. This dataset contains several
food and beverage restaurants circulating the city. By using this data set you can research any restaurant in times square
by searching up the sub-industry using the key provided. After you have searched up a sub-industry, a card will appear
containing the name, address, and the phone number of the restaurant. With this dataset you can explore many new places
to dine with just a click of a button.
